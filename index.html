Kirby–Paris Hydra (Playable Variant)

Moves: 0

Rule (variant): Click a leaf to cut it. If its parent is the root, it is simply removed. Otherwise, after removal, let P be the parent and G the grandparent. At depth d = depth(P) ≥ 1, attach C(d) copies of the subtree rooted at P to G, where C(d) starts at 1 and increases by 1 each time you cut at that depth. This variant is inspired by the Kirby–Paris hydra and still always terminates.

Tip: Start with Simple or Medium. Try to slay the hydra: remove all leaves so the root has no children. The move counter increments on each cut.
